My Experience with Archives and Digital Humanities

← Back to Blog

I have experience with archives in multiple mediums. I have been to museums to research relevant historical information for history projects and also used archive.org to access past versions of websites that may have records of information I need. Additionally, I have also read multiple textbooks that hold first or second hand accounts of historical events to educate future scholars. All these sources proved incredibly useful to gather more knowledge on a variety of topics and perpetuate information.
